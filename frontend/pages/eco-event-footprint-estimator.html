Frequently Asked Questions

How is the sustainability score calculated?
The score is based on waste, energy, transport, and other event factors. Higher recycling, renewable energy, and sustainable transport improve your score.
Can I use this tool for any event size?
Yes, the estimator is designed for events of all sizes.
How can I improve my event's score?
Increase recycling, composting, renewable energy, local sourcing, and sustainable transport options.
Is my data saved?
No, all calculations are done locally in your browser.

About This Tool

The Eco-Event Footprint Estimator was developed to help event organizers minimize environmental impact and promote sustainability. For more information, contact eco-support@example.com.
