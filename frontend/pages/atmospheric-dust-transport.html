The Invisible Fertilizer: Dust as a Global Nutrient Distributor

Atmospheric dust transport represents one of nature's most remarkable ecological phenomena. Massive dust storms originating in arid deserts can travel thousands of kilometers, depositing vital nutrients in nutrient-poor ecosystems and dramatically altering their productivity.
