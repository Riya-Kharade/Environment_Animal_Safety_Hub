Efficiency Tips

  • Upgrade insulation to reduce heating and cooling costs.
  • Install double or triple glazed windows.
  • Switch to LED lighting.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Install solar panels for renewable energy.
  • Monitor usage with a smart meter.
  • Seal gaps and cracks to prevent drafts.
  • Set thermostats to optimal temperatures.
  • Use programmable thermostats.
  • Maintain heating and cooling systems regularly.
  • Consider heat pumps for efficient heating.
  • Reduce standby power by unplugging devices.
  • Wash clothes in cold water.
  • Dry clothes naturally when possible.
  • Use curtains and blinds to manage heat.
  • Plant trees for natural shading.
  • Choose local and sustainable materials for renovations.
  • Track improvements and set goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the efficiency score calculated?
The score is based on energy usage, insulation, windows, appliances, and renewable energy.
Can I use this tool for any home?
Yes, the analyzer is designed for homes of all types and sizes.
How can I improve my score?
Upgrade insulation, windows, appliances, and increase renewable energy use.
Is my data saved?
No, all calculations are done locally in your browser.
