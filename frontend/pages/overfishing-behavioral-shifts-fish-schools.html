Selective Harvesting Mechanisms

Size-Selective Fishing

Larger, more experienced fish are preferentially removed, disrupting social hierarchies

High Impact

  • Reduced group coordination
  • Younger, less experienced schools

Personality-Driven Selection

Bold, risk-taking individuals are more likely to encounter fishing gear

High Impact

  • Altered predator avoidance
