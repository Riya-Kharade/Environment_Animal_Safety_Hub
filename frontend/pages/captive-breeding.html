Key Challenges & Solutions

Lead Poisoning

Primary cause of mortality in wild condors

Solution: Non-lead ammunition advocacy, regular blood testing and treatment
Slow Reproduction

One egg every two years, sexual maturity at 6-8 years

Solution: Double-clutching - removing first egg to stimulate second laying
Habitat Loss

Limited suitable release sites

Solution: Multiple release sites across historical range
