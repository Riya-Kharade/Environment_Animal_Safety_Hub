Epigenetic Mechanisms

DNA Methylation

Addition of methyl groups to cytosine residues, altering gene expression without changing DNA sequence

CG
  • CpG island methylation
  • Gene silencing effects
  • Transgenerational inheritance

Histone Modifications

Post-translational changes to histone proteins affecting chromatin structure and gene accessibility

  • Acetylation and methylation
  • Chromatin remodeling
  • Gene activation/repression

Non-Coding RNA

microRNAs and long non-coding RNAs that regulate gene expression at transcriptional and post-transcriptional levels

miRNA
  • miRNA interference
  • mRNA degradation
  • Translation inhibition

Chromatin Remodeling

ATP-dependent complexes that alter nucleosome positioning and chromatin structure

  • Nucleosome repositioning
  • Gene accessibility changes
  • Transcription factor binding

Pollutant-Specific Effects

Heavy Metals

Lead (Pb): Neurodevelopmental gene methylation
Mercury (Hg): Histone modifications in neural tissues
Cadmium (Cd): Global DNA hypomethylation

Endocrine Disruptors

Bisphenol A: Estrogen receptor methylation changes
Phthalates: Thyroid hormone receptor epigenetic marks
Dioxins: Aryl hydrocarbon receptor signaling disruption

Air Pollutants

PM2.5: Oxidative stress-mediated epigenetic changes
VOCs: Respiratory gene methylation alterations
Ozone: Lung tissue histone modifications

Health Consequences

Developmental Effects

Neurodevelopmental disorders, reproductive abnormalities, immune system dysregulation

Chronic Diseases

Cancer, cardiovascular disease, neurological disorders, autoimmune diseases

Transgenerational Effects

Heritable epigenetic marks affecting disease susceptibility in offspring

Research & Solutions

Biomarker Development

DNA methylation arrays, ChIP-seq analysis, RNA sequencing for epigenetic monitoring

Prevention Strategies

Pollution control, exposure reduction, regulatory frameworks, public health measures

Therapeutic Approaches

Epigenetic drugs, antioxidant therapy, nutritional interventions, lifestyle modifications
