Drivers of Biotic Homogenization

Global Trade & Transport

Human-mediated species dispersal:

  • Ships and cargo containers
  • International air travel
  • Ornamental plant trade
  • Pet and livestock transport

Climate Change

Shifting species distributions:

  • Southern species moving north
  • Urban heat island effects
  • Extended growing seasons
  • Altered migration patterns

Urban Habitat Similarity

Comparable niches worldwide:

  • Artificial structures and surfaces
  • Managed green spaces
  • Human food waste resources
  • Disturbed soil environments
