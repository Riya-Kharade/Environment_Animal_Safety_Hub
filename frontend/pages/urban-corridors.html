What are Urban Wildlife Corridors?

Safe pathways connecting green spaces in cities

Green Highways

Natural pathways that allow animals to move between habitat fragments

Safe Passage

Protected routes that reduce road casualties and human-wildlife conflict

Habitat Connectivity

Linking isolated parks and green spaces into connected networks

Types of Corridors

Different designs for different needs

Wildlife Bridges

Overpasses covered with vegetation that allow animals to cross highways safely

Large mammals Vegetated cover

Green Roof Corridors

Connected rooftop gardens that create pathways for birds and insects

Birds & insects Building networks

Underpass Tunnels

Underground passages for amphibians and small mammals to cross roads

Amphibians Protected passage
