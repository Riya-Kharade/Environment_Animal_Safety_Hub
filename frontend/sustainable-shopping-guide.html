Eco-Label Explanations

  • Fair Trade: Ensures ethical sourcing and fair wages.
  • Organic: Produced without synthetic chemicals.
  • Energy Star: Certified for energy efficiency.
  • FSC: Forest Stewardship Council certified wood/paper.
  • Recycled: Made from recycled materials.
