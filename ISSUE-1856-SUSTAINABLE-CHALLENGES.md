## Issue #1856
**Status:** Open  
**Priority:** High  
**Type:** Feature Request  
**Date Created:** February 5, 2026

## Description

Build a frontend-only **Sustainable Living Challenge Hub** where users can join weekly environmental challenges (zero-waste challenges, plastic-free shopping, bike commute weeks, vegan days, energy conservation, etc.), track completion streaks, accumulate green points, earn environmental badges, view global leaderboards, share achievements on social platforms, and see their cumulative environmental impact. Include challenge discovery, participation history, community challenges, and reward redemption system.

**Modern, engaging UI with social gamification, no backend required.**

1. **Challenge Library**
