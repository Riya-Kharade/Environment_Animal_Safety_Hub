# Carbon Footprint Calculator with AI Insights

## Issue #1855
**Status:** Open  
**Priority:** High  
**Type:** Feature Request  
**Date Created:** February 5, 2026

---

## Description

Build a frontend-only **Personal Carbon Calculator** that lets users track their daily carbon footprint across multiple categories (transportation, energy, food, shopping, waste), visualize trends, set reduction targets, receive AI-powered personalized recommendations, and compare their impact with community averages. Include monthly dashboards, historical tracking, goal progress, environmental tips, and a carbon offset guide.

**Modern UI with impressive data visualization, no backend required.**

---

## Requirements

### Core Features

1. **Carbon Footprint Calculator**
   - Multiple input categories:
     - Transportation (car, public transit, flights, biking, walking)
     - Energy (electricity, gas/heating, renewable %)
     - Food (meat/dairy intake, local vs imported, food waste)
     - Shopping (clothing, electronics, packaging)
     - Waste (recycling rate, landfill, composting)
   - Real-time impact calculations
   - Unit options (lbs, kg, tons)

2. **Daily & Monthly Dashboards**
   - Today's carbon footprint
   - Weekly/monthly trends
   - Category breakdown with visual charts
   - Comparison with previous periods
   - Personal best achievements

3. **Reduction Goals**
   - Set carbon reduction targets (% or absolute)
   - Select challenge duration (1 week to 6 months)
   - Real-time progress tracking
   - Milestone rewards and badges
   - Motivational alerts

4. **AI-Powered Recommendations**
   - Analyze top carbon contributors
   - Suggest specific actions with impact estimates
   - Difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
   - Estimated savings in CO‚ÇÇ, $ money, time
   - Quick-win recommendations

5. **Community Comparison**
   - Your carbon footprint vs community average
   - Regional leaderboard (by neighborhood/city)
   - Category-specific rankings
   - User demographic comparisons
   - Privacy-controlled visibility settings

6. **Offset Options**
   - Carbon credit calculator
   - Tree planting equivalent
   - Renewable energy projects info
   - Personal offset tracking
   - Impact visualizations

7. **Data Visualization**
   - Pie charts for category distribution
   - Line charts for trend analysis
   - Bar charts for comparisons
   - Heat maps for daily patterns
   - Tree/leaf animations for offset visualization
